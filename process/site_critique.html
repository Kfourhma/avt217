Lomex Gallery

https://www.lomex.gallery/

Navigation

Navigation is extremely easy thanks to the simple style of the website and easily accessible banner.

Usability

Every link works properly and the important ones in the banner are easily accessible. Though I do dislike the store being an entirely different website.

Typography and hierarchy

The font is simple and the arrangement is nice and centered. I do like the vertical arrangement of the website.

Overall style/look and feel

It's very minimalistic and unappealing on the front page, but once you dive into the exhibitions it showcases a lot of art and manages to slip past that boring white front page.
